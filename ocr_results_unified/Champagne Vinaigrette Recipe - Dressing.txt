RECIPE FOR: Champagne Vinaigrette Recipe - Dressing
1/3 C champagne vinegar
1/3 C fresh basil, snipped
2 TBS finely minced shallots
1 tsp Rothschild champagne. Garlic Mustard
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 C garlic flavored olive oil

Mix all ingredients together except olive oil. Slowly pour olive oil until well blended, whisking constantly. Refrigerate several hours. Shake before using. Makes/cup.
