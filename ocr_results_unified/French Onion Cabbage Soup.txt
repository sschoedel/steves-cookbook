In a large saucepan or soup pot, bring broth to a boil. Transfer cabbage & onions, along with cooking juices to boiling broth. Reduce heat to med.-low & simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes.

Drain cabbage & onions, reserving the broth. Ladle half of the cabbage & onions into a large, deep oven-proof casserole dish. Arrange 4 bread slices on top & strew with 3/4 of the cheese. Ladle 1 1/2 cups of reserved broth into casserole. Add remaining cabbage & onions on top & ladle remaining broth.

Top w/ remaining bread & cheese & dot w/ remaining butter. Bake 'til cheese has melted & crust is golden, about 15 min.

PREPARATION TIME: TOTAL 1 HR 10 MINS SERVES: A LOT
