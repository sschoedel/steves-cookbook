RECIPE FOR: Pork Noodle Soup

4-8 cups chicken Broth (cover enough to pork)
1/4 cup Soy Sauce
1/4 cup Sherry
3 TB Brown Sugar
4 cloves garlic
1 inch ginger peeled & sieved
2 pods - STAR ANUSE
Kosher Salt
3 lbs Pork Shoulde
1 head Bok Choy
3.5 oz dried rice vermicelli
1/2 cup cilantroPut all ingredients into crack pot except Bok Choy, noodles & cilantro

Cook for 8 to 10 hrs
Remove pork-shred
Add chopped Bok Choy - cook for 20 minutes
Add noodles - cook for 10
Pour pork buck in pot
Ladle into bowl, top w/ cilantro

PREPARATION TIME: 8 hrs SERVES: 4-6
