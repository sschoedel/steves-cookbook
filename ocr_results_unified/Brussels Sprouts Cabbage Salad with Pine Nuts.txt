RECIPE FOR: THE PUREST SALAD
(ROADED BRUSSELS SPROUTS W/ CABBAGE & PINENUTS
1/2 CUPPINE NUTS
1 lb BRUSSELS SPROUTS, QUARTERED
6 T XTA Virgin olive oil
salt & fresh red black pepper
3 T fresh lemon juice
2 T honey
2 t Dijon mustard
1/8 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t pure chili powder, like ANCHO
1 1/2 lb red cabbage, sliced very thin
4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1 oz. thinly shaved formigiano cheese1 Preheat oven to 450°. Spread pinenuts in pie plate & toast about 3 min., golden brown. Toss brussels sprouts w/ 1 T. oil on g baking sheet, season w/ salt & pepper. Roast 15 min. until carmelized & slightly tender. 2 In small bowl, whisk lemon juice w/ honey, mustard, cayenne & chile powder. Slowly whisk in ¼ cup olive oil & season w/ salt & pepper. 3 In 1g bowl, asscabbage w/ toasted pinenuts. In small skillet, heat remaining olive oil. Add garlic & cook over moderate heat until golden, about 1 min. Scrape garlic & hot oil over cabbage. Add brussels sprouts & toss, then add dressing & toss again. Scatter cheese shavings & serve right away.

PREPARATION TIME: 25 TOTAL: 40 SERVES: 4
