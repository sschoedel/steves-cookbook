RECIPE FOR: CABBAGE SOUP

2 TBS butter 4 cups vegetable broth
1 small head cabbage 6 slices day-old, dark
cored & coarsely chopped bread - rye or pumpernickel
2 yellow onions, chopped 1/4 lb medium-dry cheese,
1 tsp. salt such as Cantal, Edam,
2 tsp. black pepper or Gruyere, grated
Preheat oven to 375Â° F.

In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 1/2 TBS butter. Add cabbage & onions & salt & pepper. Reduce heat to med.-low, cover, & cook until cabbage has wilted & reduced in volume, 5 or 6 minutes. Stir & continue to cook another 5 or 6 min.
