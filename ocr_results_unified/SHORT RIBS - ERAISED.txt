RECIPE FOR: SHORT RIBS - ERAISED

ING: 4 lbs short ribs flanken style
5 dP 3 sprigs fresh thyme dftskm
3 c. ZINFANDEL 2 bay leaves
1/2 c. sugar 3 org Canola
6 org can chopped tomatoes 1 lg onion diced
2 c beef broth 2 med carrots diced
1 TB minced garlic 2 org dried porcin
2 st celery diced

1. Season ribs w/ 2 dP. In mixing bowl, wisk rin,
sugar, tomatoes & broth & garlic & thyme
& bay leaves & pinch of salt

2. Pour Canola oil into heavy pot. Brown the meat.

3. Remove beef after browning. Add onions, carrots & celery allowing onion to cook til brown, about 10 min.
4. Return beef to pot w/ wine mixture; allow wine to boil before reducing heat, skimming fat from surface. After simmering several minutes, add mushrooms. Cover & simmer over very low heat until meat is tender, 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
5. Once ribs are cooked, remove from pot & keep warm; turn up heat & reduce liquids until thickened, about 10 minutes. Season w/ S&P
6. Transfer ribs to shallow bowl; spoon liquid over top.

PREPARATION TIME: 3hrs SERVES: 4 people
