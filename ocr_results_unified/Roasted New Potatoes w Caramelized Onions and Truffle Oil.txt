RECIPE FOR: Roasted New Potatoes w/ Caramelized Onions & Truffle Oil

1 lb small, new potatoes, scrubbed & quartered
1 1/2 yellow onion, peeled & thinnly sliced
3 TBS olive oil
Kosher salt & freshly ground black pepper
several shakes of white truffle oil, about 1/2 tsp.

Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Put onions & potatoes in a sturdy roasting pan. Pour olive oil over them & toss well to coat. Liberally sprinkle salt & pepper over potatoes & onions. Spread

potatoes out so they are in a single layer in the pan. Place in oven. Cook for 40 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned & cooked through.
