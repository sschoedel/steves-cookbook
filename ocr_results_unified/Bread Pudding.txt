RECIPE FOR: Bread Pudding
Butter for greasing
1 lb brioche, crust removed, torn into 1-inch pieces
1 1/2 pints vanilla bean ice cream
4 lg eggs, beaten
1 cup sugar
preheat 350Â° (350)
Butter shallow, 2-quart baking dish & spread the
torn brioche in it. Scoop ice cream in a glass bowl
& warm in microwave in 20 sec bursts, stirring,
just until melted. Whisk in the eggs, sugar &
3/4 cup of water. Pour custard over brioche &
let stand for 15 min, pressing to submerge bread.

2. Bake bread pudding for 30 to 35 min. til top is browned. Let cool slightly.

3. Meanwhile make caramel sauce in a med. sauce pan combine sugar w/ 2 T water. Cook over moderate heat, swirling pan & brushing down side w/ a wet pastry brush until sugar dissolves. Cook undisturbed until a medium-amber caramel forms, about 5 minutes. Add the cream (be careful, it may boil rigorously) & simmer, whisking, until the caramel is smooth, 1 to 2. Let cool slightly, then stir in whiskey & salt.

* Ingredients for caramel sauce: 2/3 C. sugar; 1/2 C. heavy cream, room temp; 1 T whiskey; pinch sea salt

SERVE WARM BREAD PUDDING W/ SAUCE
