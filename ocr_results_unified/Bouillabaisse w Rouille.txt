RECIPE FOR: Bouillabaisse w/ Rouille

- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1 cup @ chopped onion & leek
- 4 cloves mashed garlic
- Can whole tomatoes
- 2 1/2 qu. water
- 1/2 teaspoon saffron
- fresh herb sprigs: thyme, parsley, fennel fronds, basil
- 1 Tblspn sea salt
- 3-4 lbs fishheads, trimmings
- toasted rustic bread
- Rouille sauce (recipe follows)
- 1 1/2 lbs @ peeled shrimp, wild cod, halibut, sole, scrubbed mussels or clams (use shells, trimmings for stock)

Heat oil in tall pot overmed. heat; add onion & leek & cook gently until softened. Stir in garlic & cook 1 minute, then add tomatoes, water, herbs, saffron, salt & fish bones (shrimp shells) bring to simmer, then reduce heat so broth bubbles

slowly without boiling. Cook 30 min. Strain broth into 1g bowl & discard solids. Pour broth in stock ppt & bring to boil. Add shrimp & cook until pink (1 min. or 2). Add rest of fish & shellfish, cover & simmer until mussels or clams open. Add salt & ground pepper if needed. Serve w/ toast & Rouille aside

**Rouille**
- 1 roasted & peeled red bell pepper
- 1 roasted hot red chili pepper
- 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 potato cooked in soup
- 1 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil

Slowly add olive oil while processing to form paste.
