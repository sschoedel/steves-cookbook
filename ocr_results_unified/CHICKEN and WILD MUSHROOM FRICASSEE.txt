W/ CREAMY TURNIPS

RECIPE FOR: CHICKEN & WILD MUSHROOM FRICASSEE

CHICKEN
1/4 C. OLIVE OIL 1 TBS-TOMATO PASTE
6 CHICK. THIGHS W/SKIN 1/4 tsp CAYENNE PEPPER
KOSHER SALT & BLACK PEPPER 1/2 C DRY WHITE WINE
1 ONION, CHOPPED 1 BAY LEAF
1 CELERY RIB, CHOPPED 2 THYME SPRIGS
2 GARLIC CLOVES, THINLY SLICED 1 CARROT, CHOPPED
4 OZ WILD MUSHROOM, CHOPPED 4 C LOW-SODIUM CHICKEN BROTH

TURNIP PUREE
4 small turnips (1 1/2 lb) peeled & quartered
1 large Yukon Gold potato (8 oz) " & "
1 Rosemary sprig / Kosher salt / 1/4 C heavy cream
6 TBS unsalted butter / fresh ground black pepper

DSS

In 19 cast iron casserole, heat oil until shimmering. Season chicken with salt & pepper. Work in batches browning chicken over moderately high heat, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer to plate.

Add onion, carrot & celery to casserole & cook over moderate heat stirring occasionally until lightly browned, 7 to 8 min. Add garlic & mushrooms & cook 2 min. Stir in tomato paste & cayenne & cook stirring until deep mahogany, about 5 min. Add wine, bay leaf & thyme & simmer for 2 min. Add broth & chicken & bring to a boil. Cover & simmer over moderate heat until chicken is tended, ~1 hour.

TURNIP PUREE: IN MED. SAUCEPAN COMBINE TURNIPS & POTATOES w/ rosemary & water to cover by 1 inch. Season w/salt & bring to a boil. Simmer vegetables over moderate heat until tender, 20-25 min. Drain veggies & discard rosemary. Transfer turnips & potato to a food processor, add cream & butter & puree until smooth. Season turnip puree w/salt & pepper & keep warm.

Transfer chicken to a plate. Boil the cooking liquid until thickened & reduce to 2 1/2 cups, about 10 min. Discard bay leaf & thyme. Coarsely shred the chicken & stir it into reduced sauce, discard skin & bones. Simmer gently until chicken is warmed through, about 2 minutes. Serve chicken fricasee w/ creamy turnips.

Fricasee & Turnip Puree can be refrigerated separately for up to 2 days. Reheat gently

PREPARATION TIME: ACTIVE 1 HR SERVES: 60
TOTAL 2 HRS Made in China
