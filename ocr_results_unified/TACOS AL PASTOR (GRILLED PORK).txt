RECIPE FOR: TACOS AL PASTOR (GRILLED PORK)

4 DRIED GUASILLO CHILES (ABOUT 1 OZ) 1 WHOLE CLOVE
1 DRIED ANCHO CHILE 1/2 TBS BROWN SUGAR
2 DRIED CHIPOTLE CHILES 1/4 tsp GARLIC POWDER
2 TBS fresh orange juice 2 TBS veggie oil
1/2 TBS fresh lime juice 2 1/2 lb BONELESS PORK SHOULDER
1/4 C COCA COLA
3 TBS Cider vinegar 1 1g SPANISH ONION - 1/2" SLICES
1 tsp ANNAI TO SEEDS 5 A LET & FRESH GROUND PEPPER
1/2 tsp DRIED OREGANO 12 CORN TORTILTS, WARMED
1/4 tsp CUMIN SEEDS 1/2 GRILLED PINETAPLE, CHOPPED FOR SERVING
* 3 REDONION & CILANTEO

â‘  STEAM & SEED ALL DRIED CHILES & PLACE IN MICROWAVE - SAFE BOWL. COVER W/WATER & MICROWAVE AT HIGH

UNTIL SOFTENED, ABOUT 3 MIN. LET COOL SLIGHTLY, DRAIN & TRANSFER TO BLENDER. ADD ORANGE JUICE, LIME JUICE, SODA & VINEGAR. IN SPICE GRINDER, GRIND ANNATTO W/OREGANO, CUMIN, CLOVE, SUGAR & GARLIC POWDER UNTIL FINE. ADD SPICE MIXTURE TO BLENDER & BLEND UNTIL SMOOTH. 2. TRANSFER MARINADE TO SAUCEPAN. ADD 2 TBS OIL & LOOK OVER MOD. HEAT UNTIL VERY THICK (5 MIN). LET COOL. TRANSFER MARINADE TO LG, RESEALABLE & AGA. & MARINADE PORK & ONION IN REF. OVERNIGHT. 3. SCRAPE OFF MOST OF MARINADE. BRUSH PORK & ONION W/ OIL & SEASON W/ S&P. GRILL ABOUT 15 MIN. COVER W/ LOOSE FOIL ON WORK STATION ABOUT 10 MIN. 4. CUT PORK INTO STRIPS. SERVE PORK & ONION W/ WARMED TORTIUTS. GRILLED PINEAPPLE, RED ONION & CILANTRO.

PREPARATION TIME: TOTAL: 1 HR 15 MIN. SERVES: 12 TACOS
