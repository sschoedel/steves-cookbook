RECIPE FOR: Bacon Wrapped Shrimp w/ Grits

FOR SHRIMP: 18 pc black tiger shrimp, peeled & deceived
1/2 T Kosher salt
9 bacon slices, cut in half
2 t cracked black pepper
Grape seed oil for Searing

FOR GRITS: 2 qt chicken stock 1 qt milk
1/4 stick butter 1 qt instant grits
2 cups grilled corn kernels 3 T chopped cilandro
1 cup poblano peppers, diced 2 T cracked bl. pepper
2 cups aged white cheddar cheese

FOR CHI POTLE - TOMATO BUTTER: 1/2 yellow onion, chopped

2 T chopped garlic 3 ROMA TOMATOES, CHOPPED, (OVER)

3T CHIPOTLE PEPPERS (in Adobo) 2c chicken stock
1/2 black peppercorns 1/2 stick butter
1/2 bunch cilantro 1 lemon, juiced

Shrimp Prep:
Season shrimp w/a little Kosher salt. Using a hot grill, quickly mark @ shrimp. Remove from grill & allow to cool. Shrimp should still be uncooked. When cool enough to handle, wrap @ shrimp w/a half piece bacon. Season w/ cracked black pepper. Sear @ shrimp in grapeseed oil til crispy on both sides. Serve when rest of dish is ready.

RECIPE FOR: Bacon wrapped Shrimp w/Grits.. contd

Grits prep:

Using a deep saucepan, combine chicken stock & milk. Bring to a simmer, add butter, & allow time for it to dissolve. Slowly whisk in grits a little at a time. When grits begin to thicken, lower heat. Switch to a spatula & fold in the rest of the ingredients. Adjust for additional seasonings if needed. If grits are too thick, add more chicken stock.

For Chipotle-Tomato Butter Sauce:

In a sauce pot, saute onions & garlic

over medium heat. Next, add tomatoes, chipotle peppers & peppercorns. Continue to cook for another 3 minutes. Deglaze with chicken stock & lower heat. Cook 5 min, then place in a blender; blend til smooth. Place liquid back in saucepan & over low heat - mount w/butter, whisking in a little at a time. Add cilantro & season w/ lemon juice & salt. Turn off heat & allow 5 minutes to steep cilantro. Strain & reserve warm til time to serve. Place grits in a bowl, drizzle sauce around grits, shrimp on grits.

PREPARATION TIME: 1 hr 15 min. SERVES: 6
