RECIPE FOR: Roasted Chicken w/ apples & mixed veggies

8 chicken thighs 12 1/2 garlic cloves, peeled
8 chicken drums 1 1/2 yellow onion, julienned
Salt & pepper to taste 1 1/2 apple, chopped
1/4 C olive oil 1 pint Brussels sprouts, halved
4 large carrots, sliced

In large skillet, season chicken with salt & pepper & olive oil. Sear in hot pan to brown outside & place entire pan with contents in the oven. Bake at 375Â°F. Veggies will cook in varying times, so be aware not to add them too early, or they will overcook. When chicken has been in the oven 15 minutes, add carrots & garlic cloves. After another 15 min,

add onions, apples, & Brussels sprouts. Veggies & chicken should be fully cooked after 45 minutes, but check thighs to make sure.

PREPARATION TIME: 1 hr 30 min. SERVES: 8
