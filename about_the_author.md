  Version 1 (Warm & Narrative):

  Steve was born in 1964 in Richmond, Virginia, as the middle child in a loving family that taught him the value of hard work and togetherness. A natural leader from an early age, he
   served as president of his high school before heading to Dartmouth, where he honed his passion for business and economics. Throughout his life, Steve has been driven by the thrill
   of building something new—particularly in the risky, rewarding world of restaurants. But beyond the business ventures and entrepreneurial spirit, food has always been his greatest
   joy. For Steve, cooking isn't just about the meal; it's about gathering the people he loves around the table and sharing something meaningful. This collection represents exactly
  that: 100 recipes that have brought his family together, one dish at a time.
