RECIPE FOR: Tambalaya

12 Shrimp (peeled & deceived)
4 oz chicken 2 2 1/2 teas. paprika
1 TB Creole Seasoning K 2 TB salt
2 TB Olive oil 1 TB black pepper
1/4 cup chopped onion 1 TB Onion powder
1/4 cup chopped green pepper 1 TB Cayenne Pepper
1/2 cup chopped celery 1 TB oregano
2 TB chopped garlic 1 TB dried Thyme
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon hot sauce
3/4 cup rice
3 cups Chicken Stock

DSS502 and Quille sausage sliced
Soup
Combine shrimp, chicken & creole seasoning - work in well. In a large saucepan, heat oil w/ onions, peppers & celery 3 minutes. Add garlic, tomatoes, buy leaves, Wore. & hot sauces. Shr in rice & slowly add broth. Reduce heat to medium & cook until rice absorbs liquid stirring occasionally (about 15 minutes). When rice is tender, add shrimp & chicken & sausage. Cook til done (10 minutes). Season to taste.
