RECIPE FOR: CLASSIC CHICKEN SOUP

4 lb chicken, quartered 4 lg parsley sprigs
3 qt cold water 1 TBS Kosher salt
6 large carrots, thinly sliced 1/4 tsp ground pepper
3 large celery stalks, sliced 1/2 lb med. egg noodles
3 small parsnips, peeled, sliced 2 TBS minced parsley
1 lg onion, cut into 8 wedges

In a stock pot, bring chicken & water to a boil, skimming foam. Add all but 2 cups of carrots and celery, parsnips, onion, parsley sprigs, 2 tsp of salt & pepper. Simmer, partially covered, until chicken is cooked, about 1 hour. Remove chicken to a plate. When cool enough to handle, remove skin and bones, discard. Shred meat,

Set aside.

Strain broth through cheesecloth-lined sieve into pot (5 gf). Press solids to extract liquid. Add remaining carrots & salt. Simmer covered, 5 minutes. Add noodles, simmer covered, 7 minutes or until carrots are tender and noodles are cooked. Add chicken & minced parsley. Heat through.

PREPARATION TIME:
SERVES: 8
