RECIPE FOR: VAPPARDELLE w/ WHITE BOLGENSE

|  3 TBS extra virgin olive oil | 1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper corns  |
| --- | --- |
|  1 lb ground veal, pork or beef | pinch crushed pepper  |
|  1/2 c. dry white wine | pinch freshly grated nutmeg  |
|  1 1/4 c half & half | kosher salt & fresh ground pepper  |
|  1 c chicken stock | 1/4 c finely chopped bacon  |
|  1 thyme sprig | 1 c finely chopped onion  |
|  2 sage leaves | 1/4 c. " " white or creminimushone  |
|  1 bay leaf | 1/4 c. " " peeled celery root  |
|  1 sm garlic clove, minced | 1/2 lb pappardelle  |
|  freshly grated Parmigiano cheese for serving |   |

1- 1n alarge enamelled cast-iron casserole, heat olive

DSS

oil until shimmering. Add veal & pork & cook over moderately high heat, stirring occasionally, until most of the liquid has evaporated & the meat is nearly cooked throughout, 6-8 min. Add wine & cook over moderate heat, scraping any browned bits, about 3 min.
2- Add half & half & chicken stock to casserole, stir in thyme, rosemary & sage, bay leaf, pink pepper, crushed red pepper, nutmeg & generous pinch of salt & pepper. Bring just to a simmer. Cover & cook over low heat, stir occasionally, until sauce thickens & meat is tender, 2 hrs.
3- in med. skillet, cook bacon over mod. heat, stir until fat has rendered, 5 min. Add onion, mushrins, & celery root, stir until soffitto into Bolognese sauce, cover partially & cook over mod. low heat, stir occasionally, until sauce reduces slightly, 25 min. x

PREPARATION TIME: 30 min / Cook time: 3 hrs
SERVES: 2+

Discard the thyme, rosemary & bay leaf. Season the Bolognese sauce w/ salt & pepper & keep warm over very low heat.

4- In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the pappardelle until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup of the cooking water. Add the pasta & cooking water to the Bolognese sauce & toss over moderate heat until the pasta is well coated, 2 min. Transfer pasta to a large, shallow bowl & serve right away,
