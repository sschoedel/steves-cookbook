2) Add chicken & cook over medium heat 3 minutes. Add mushrooms & cook another 2 minutes. Add tomato & tomato paste & stir until combined. Stir in wine & bring to a boil. Reduce heat & simmer until liquid is reduced by half.

3) Stir in the cream & salt & pepper, to taste, & bring to a boil. Reduce heat & simmer until sauce begins to thicken.

4) While sauce is cooking, cook penne in a large pan of rapidly boiling salted water until al dente. Drain well & return to pan. Add sauce to pasta & toss. Serve immediately, sprinkled with Parmesan.

PREPARATION TIME: 30 MIN. SERVES: 4
COOKING TIME: 25 MIN.
