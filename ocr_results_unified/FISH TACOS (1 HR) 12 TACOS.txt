RECIPE FOR: FISH TACOS (1 HR) 12 TACOS

1/2 c mayo
1/4 c hoisin sauce
2 TBS SWEET PICKLE RELISH
1 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
1 c flour
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 Tbs kosher salt
1 tsp white pepper
1-1 lb tilapia fillets, cut into strips
2 lg eggs, beaten
3 C Danko (Japanese bread crumbs)
3 c veggie oil, for frying
12 corn tortillas, warmed
* Shredded cabbage (green)
1 lettuce leaves, cilantro &
1 sliced scallions, for serving

(1 In bowl, whisk mayow/hoisin sauce, pickle relish & lemon juice. (2 In 1g resealable plastic bag, combine flour, garlic powder, onion powder, salt & white pepper. Seal

bag & shake. Add fish & shake to coat. ③ Put eggs & panko in separate shell on bowls. Dip fish in egg & then in panko. Transfer fish to a wax paper-lined platter. ④ In 1g, deep skillet, heat oil to 350°. Add half fish & fry over moderate heat, turning once, until golden, about 3 minutes. Drain fish on paper towel lined platter. Repeat w/remaining fish. Serve fish in tortillas, passing hoisin mayo, cabbage, lettuce & cilantro & scallions for serving. Cocktail: Margarita made w/Patron Silver.

PREPARATION TIME: 1 hour SERVES: 12 tacos
