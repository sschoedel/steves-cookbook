RECIPE FOR: BB's Apple Cake

|  4 large Apples | 1/2 tsp salt  |
| --- | --- |
|  3 tsp Cinnamon | 7 TBS orange juice  |
|  1/2 Cup Sugar | 2 1/2 tsp vanilla  |
|  2 1/4 cups Sugar | 3 cups flour  |
|  4 eggs | 3 tsp baking powder  |
|  1 cup Oil |   |

Pare & slice apples thin; add Cinnamon & 1/2 Cup Sugar. Set aside. Dump dry ingredients into large mixing bowl. Add liquid ingredients. Beat until Smooth. (Batter will be thick & heavy). Grease a Burd t or type pan & flour pan. Alternate batter & apples, starting and ending with batter. Bake

at 350Â° for one hour and 10 minutes or until done (generally it takes longer).
